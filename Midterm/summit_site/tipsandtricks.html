Tips for Training

Training your dog will be challenging but following these couple of easy tips can make training your dog easy.

  1. They need structure: These dogs will act like other dogs but it might take a little bit longer to train them. It is good to start at a young age and continue for a long while.
  2. They get bored: Remember that when training your dog that it will get bored after a long period of training. Take breaks and do it in moderation every other day.
  3. Declare right away who is that Alpha: Alaskan dogs have a higherarchy and they will stick to it. If they see you as lower in the pecking order they will not listen to you.

Grooming

Alaskan dogs have a long coat of double layer, thick, long fur that will easily get caught, tangled and matted so brushing is essential. They tend to shed there fur many times a year so you will need to get it brushed and trimmed often.

Health and Nutrition

The type of food that you should feed your Alaskan dog may vary from dog to dog but one thing will be consistent. Make sure they are feed 2 to 3 times per day. These dogs are working dogs and will need food for the energy they burn.

Make sure the food that you feed them is a meat based food of any sort that also has some sort of veteable incorperated. You must make sure that they dont over eat or they will start to put on weight and get slower and lazier.
